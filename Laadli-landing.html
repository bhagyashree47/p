LAADLI - A Girl Child Campaign

Laadli, a girl child campaign of Population First, was launched in June 2005 to address the problem of the falling sex ratio highlighted in the Census of 2001. However, over the years the scope of the project was redefined to work with media, advertising and youth to address the reasons why she is considered unwanted and undermined in our society.
